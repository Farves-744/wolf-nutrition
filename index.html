Wolf Nutrition was established in early 2016 by psychology graduate Abdullah Al Tarrak as Kuwait’s first ketogenic meal plan. We started out small not accepting any more than 50 subscribers to ensure quality maintenance. With demand and awareness of the Ketogenic diet spreading in Kuwait as well as the overwhelming results, we expanded our company to grow with the demand. Wolf Nutrition adopts a philosophy of self empowerment, through self awareness, and self challenge. We believe we, each of us, individually, are the agents of our lives and manifestors of destiny. Conquering our physical goals and challenges is a fundamental launching stone to achieving the best possible version of who we might potentially become.
